### ACT I — MIDDAY CONFESSION

March 13th, 2026. Main branch, 12:32 PM IST. The TODO.md file sat in the root like a confession waiting to be filed. Ten lines of scattered ambition had become three hundred and thirty-eight lines of architectural obsession.

### ACT II — THE SCHEMA EMERGES

Omkar against himself—the builder against the director. He was no longer writing features; he was designing the pipeline that would generate the stories of features. The HaikuDirector.md prompt, the JSON contract, the DB schema mutations—each one a lock clicking into place on a cage he was building for his own output.

### ACT III — THE AUTOMATION TRAP

He mapped out _generate_haiku_batch, _write_cinematic_markdown, the ALTER TABLE commands for title, subtitle, verdict columns. He sketched the decade-compilation logic, the PyQt rendering, the full-screen slide machinery. Every function name was a confession: he was automating the very act of confession itself. The app no longer needed him to write the stories; it needed him to build the machine that would write them while he slept. He had become the director of his own obsolescence.
